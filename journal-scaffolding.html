Volume Two

The Wide Path

Thus says The Lord: Peoples of the earth, why have you forsaken Me, in favor of broad paths that lead to your own destruction?

Today's reading struck me deeply. The Lord speaks of the way that seems right to a man, but the end thereof is destruction. I've been thinking about how often I walk that path without realizing — choosing what feels right, what feels comfortable, what looks like wisdom from where I'm standing.

The verse that kept returning was Proverbs 14:12, and reading it with the letter open beside me, I see now that the Lord has been gently pointing me to this for weeks.

The path I walked this morning — a fitting reminder.
Therefore, turn from this wide path, return to the narrow way, the way of righteousness…

The Wide Path

From The Lord, Our God and Savior · 3/28/05

Thus says The Lord: Peoples of the earth, why have you forsaken Me, in favor of broad paths that lead to your own destruction? Why do you build your houses upon the sand, when I have given you the Rock?

The way that seems right to a man — its end is the way of death. I have spoken plainly through My servants, yet you would not listen. I have called, yet you would not answer. Return to Me, and I will return to you.

❖ ❖ ❖

Therefore, turn from this wide path, return to the narrow way, the way of righteousness, that you may live and not perish in the day of My fierce anger.
